Rudolph Game

You can be a colorful Rudolph!
This game recognizes your face and make you to become a Rudolph.

Game Rules:
First, you have to click the red or green button.
If you click the left button, the nose of Rudolph goes to the left, and if you click the right button, the nose goes to the right.
Also, the color of the nose can be changed if you click the green button or red button. It's up to you!
